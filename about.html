Our Mission

QuickCab was founded in 2020 with a simple idea: to make city transportation reliable, affordable, and accessible for everyone. What started as a small startup in Bangalore has now grown into one of India's most trusted ride-hailing platforms.

We believe that everyone deserves a safe and comfortable ride, whether it's a quick trip across town or a long journey to another city. Our technology connects millions of riders with thousands of driver partners, creating economic opportunities and making cities more accessible.

Today, we operate in 25+ cities across India, with a fleet of over 10,000 vehicles and a community of 50,000+ driver partners. But we're just getting started.
