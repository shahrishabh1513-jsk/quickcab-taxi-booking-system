About RT QuickCab

We're on a mission to make getting around Vadodara simple, fair and reliable.

Our mission

Built in Vadodara, for Vadodara

RT QuickCab began in 2024 with a simple idea: give this city a ride-booking platform that actually understands its streets — from the palace roads of Fatehgunj to the industrial lanes of Makarpura.

We believe every rider deserves a fair, upfront fare and every driver-partner deserves dignity and steady earnings. Our live-map technology connects riders with nearby drivers in seconds, with zero guesswork on price.

Today we serve 40+ localities across Vadodara, with a growing fleet of autos, minis, sedans and SUVs — and we're just getting started.
