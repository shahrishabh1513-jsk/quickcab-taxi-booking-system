Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ride?

Head to the "Book Ride" page, drop your pickup and drop pins on the map, choose a vehicle, and confirm payment. Your driver is assigned instantly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), all major credit/debit cards, and cash paid directly to your driver.

How do I become a driver partner?

Click "Register as driver" on our Register page, fill in your details and documents, and our team verifies you within 24–48 hours.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancel free within 5 minutes of booking. After that, a ₹50 cancellation fee may apply.
