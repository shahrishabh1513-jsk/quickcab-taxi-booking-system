Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ride?

You can book a ride through our website by clicking on "Book Ride" and entering your pickup and drop locations. You can also download our mobile app from Google Play or App Store for easier booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, and cash. You can also add money to your QuickCab wallet for faster payments.

How do I become a driver partner?

Click on "Driver Registration" in the menu, fill out the form with your details and documents. Our team will verify your documents and activate your account within 24-48 hours.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel within 5 minutes of booking without any charge. After that, a cancellation fee of ₹50 may apply depending on the time of cancellation.
